<p><strong>Parent Support Group (PSG)</strong>
</p>
<p>The Parent Support Group (PSG) plays a vital role in building strong partnerships
between the school and the parent community. As valued partners in education,
PSG members work closely with the school to foster open communication and
encourage parent involvement in supporting our students’ learning, well-being,
and character development. Together, we aim to nurture a generation of
Together, we aim to nurture a generation of leaders of character and learners
for life.</p>
<p><strong>What we do</strong>
</p>
<p>In West Spring Secondary School, our PSG actively supports school events
such as the Secondary One Parent Engagement sessions and the Annual Cross
Country event. Through these initiatives, parents contribute to creating
a positive environment that supports students’ learning and well-being.</p>
<p>Our PSG EXCO also curates and plans meaningful activities for our parents
to engage their children and our community.</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Weekly sports sessions are held
in our school’s indoor sports hall to provide an opportunity for parents
to connect with their children.</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Participation in community initiatives
such as the Good Grocery Truck programme, in partnership with Hao Ren Hao
Shi and Zhenghua Community Club, to support residents in need by distributing
essential groceries.</p>
<p>Through these efforts, the PSG helps strengthen the school–home partnership,
supporting the holistic development of every West Spring student.</p>
